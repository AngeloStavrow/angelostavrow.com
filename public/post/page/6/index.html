Or, just strive to be the best

2 May, 2014 at 12:00 EDT

“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.” I’m not sure to whom the above quote is attributed, but I don’t buy it. The only way to do great work is to put in 100% of your attention and your effort, follow a thorough PDCA1 methodology, and understand that the quality of your work depends entirely on your will. I get that this quote is supposed to be an inspirational call to action, but thinking that you can only excel at things that you love is equal parts reductive and defeatist.
